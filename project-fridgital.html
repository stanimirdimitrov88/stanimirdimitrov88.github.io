Fridgital

A smart app to reduce food waste with receipt OCR, inventory tracking, and smart suggestions.

Overview

Fridgital digitizes grocery receipts, automatically updates your pantry inventory, and surfaces recipe ideas or restock reminders before items expire.

Flutter Firebase Cloud Functions OCR
GitHub Repository

Features

  • Receipt OCR with product parsing and unit normalization.
  • Inventory tracking with quantity and expiration management.
  • Recipe suggestions based on current stock + shopping list generation.
  • Push notifications for expiring items.
