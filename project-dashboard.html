Interactive Dashboard for Work-Related Injuries

November 2024 - February 2025

Overview

Developed an interactive dashboard targeted at NGOs advocating for workplace safety in the USA. The dashboard assists in revealing high-risk sectors, seasonal trends, and legislative gaps for data-driven policy recommendations.

Python Dashboard Data Visualization Policy Analysis

Key Features

  • Interactive visualizations revealing high-risk sectors across industries.
  • Seasonal trend analysis to identify patterns in workplace injuries.
  • Legislative gap analysis to support data-driven policy recommendations.
  • Designed specifically for NGO advocacy and workplace safety improvement.
  • User-friendly interface for exploring complex safety datasets.

Impact

  • Enables NGOs to identify priority areas for workplace safety advocacy.
  • Supports evidence-based policy recommendations with visual data insights.
  • Highlights seasonal patterns that can inform prevention campaigns.
  • Reveals legislative gaps requiring regulatory attention.
