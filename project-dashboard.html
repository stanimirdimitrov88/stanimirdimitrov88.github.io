Interactive Dashboard for Work-Related Injuries

November 2024 - February 2025

Developed an interactive dashboard targeted at NGOs advocating for workplace safety in the USA. The dashboard assists in revealing high-risk sectors, seasonal trends, and legislative gaps for data-driven policy recommendations. Interactive visualizations reveal high-risk sectors across industries. Seasonal trend analysis identifies patterns in workplace injuries. Legislative gap analysis supports data-driven policy recommendations. Designed specifically for NGO advocacy and workplace safety improvement with a user-friendly interface for exploring complex safety datasets.
