Chest X-ray Classification

Deep learning for thoracic disease detection

Fine-tuned Deep Convolutional Neural Networks for classification of thoracic diseases from chest X-ray scans. Implemented data augmentation techniques and utilized two pre-trained models: DenseNet121 and ResNet50. Fine-tuned pre-trained DenseNet121 and ResNet50 architectures with comprehensive data augmentation techniques. Conducted thorough performance evaluation comparing both models and applied transfer learning strategies leveraging ImageNet weights. Analyzed potential shortcut bias, cognitive bias, and uncertainty in predictions. Documented limitations and appropriate clinical use cases.
